Tax Services

Complete guide to income tax, VAT, TIN registration, and tax return filing in Bangladesh

Types of Taxes

Income Tax

Tax on personal and business income

  • Individual taxpayers
  • Corporate entities
  • Partnership firms

Value Added Tax (VAT)

Tax on goods and services

  • Standard rate: 15%
  • Business registration required
  • Monthly return filing

Corporate Tax

Tax on company profits

  • Public companies: 25%
  • Private companies: 30%
  • Listed companies: 20%
