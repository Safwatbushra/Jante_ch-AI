National ID (NID) Services

Complete information about National Identity Card application, correction, and verification processes in Bangladesh

What is National ID (NID)?

The National Identity Card (NID) is an official identity document for Bangladeshi citizens. It serves as proof of citizenship and identity, required for various government and private services.

For Citizens

Available for all Bangladeshi citizens aged 18 and above

Secure

Features biometric data and advanced security measures

Digital

Available in both physical and digital formats

Services Available

New Application

Apply for your first National ID card

  • Birth certificate required
  • Passport-size photos
  • Proof of address

Correction

Correct information on existing NID

  • Name correction
  • Date of birth update
  • Address change

Duplicate Copy

Get a duplicate copy if lost or damaged

  • Police report for lost card
  • Affidavit required
  • Biometric verification

Verification

Verify NID information online

  • Online verification
  • SMS verification
  • QR code scanning
