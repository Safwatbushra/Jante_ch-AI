Driving License Services

Complete guide to obtaining, renewing, and managing your driving license in Bangladesh through BRTA

Types of Driving Licenses

Professional License

For commercial vehicle drivers including buses, trucks, and taxis

  • Age requirement: 20+ years
  • Educational qualification required
  • Valid for commercial driving

Non-Professional License

For private vehicle owners and personal use

  • Age requirement: 18+ years
  • For cars, motorcycles, scooters
  • Cannot drive commercial vehicles

Application Process

1

Medical Certificate

Obtain medical fitness certificate from authorized doctor

2

Driving Training

Complete training from BRTA approved driving school

3

Theory Test

Pass written examination (60% marks required)

4

Practical Test

Pass practical driving test at BRTA office

5

Fee Payment

Pay license fee and collect your driving license

Required Documents

Essential Documents

  • National ID card (original + copy)
  • Medical certificate (valid 6 months)
  • Training certificate from approved center
  • Passport-size photographs (3 copies)

For Professional License

  • Character certificate
  • Educational certificate (Class 8 minimum)
  • Experience certificate (if applicable)
  • Previous license (for renewal)
